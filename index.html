Process

Purchase Order Process

Follow these steps to place and track your orders.

1

Send the Purchase Order

Send the purchase order to Juan Cuartas, always copying Claudia Díaz and Pilar Fernández. You may use your own purchase order format. Please ensure it includes:

  • Product name
  • Quantity
  • Price per unit
  • Buyer and seller information: company name, address, and contact details
  • Required arrival date at the destination port
Note: All quotations are made on FOB terms, so please reflect FOB prices on the purchase order. Once we receive the freight cost from the carrier, it will be included in the proforma invoice and final invoice.
Important: Let us know if you prefer to use your own carrier or if you would like us to assign one. Using a new carrier may add approximately 7 additional days to the process.
2

Label Approval Process

Once the purchase order is received, our regulatory team will send you the proposed label for confirmation. This approval is required for each purchase order, even if the label has been approved in previous shipments. Once approved, manufacturing will begin.

3

Logistics Coordination

Our logistics team will keep you informed about reservation details and provide all necessary shipping documents.

4

Shipping and Tracking

a. Full Container Load (FCL)

You will receive a tracking number and a link to monitor the shipment status.

b. Less than Container Load (LCL)

Since this type of load changes containers during transit, detailed tracking is not possible. Our logistics team will stay in contact with the carrier and keep you informed.

Lead Times

Delivery Lead Times

Shipment Type Preparation Time Transit Time Total Estimated
Sea Freight 23 - 26 days 60 - 80 days 83 - 106 days
Air Freight 23 - 26 days 7 - 10 days 30 - 36 days

Important Notes

  • Plan your orders in advance, taking into account the estimated lead times, to avoid possible delays.
  • All products shipped internationally are made to order, meaning they are manufactured from scratch after confirmation.
  • Once we complete your first shipments, we will be able to provide you with more accurate lead time estimates based on your specific logistics flow.
